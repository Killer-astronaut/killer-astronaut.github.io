Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: Lokei collects absolutely nothing. Your conversations, your data, and your activity never leave your Mac. There are no servers, no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. This is private AI the way it should be.

No Data Collection

Lokei does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. The app operates entirely on your local machine.

Specifically, Lokei never collects:

  • Conversation history — your chats are stored only on your Mac
  • Personal information — we don't know who you are
  • Usage analytics — we don't track how you use the app
  • Device information — we collect no data about your hardware or software
  • Crash reports — diagnostic data is never transmitted
  • Location data — Lokei never accesses your location

How Lokei Works

Lokei communicates exclusively with Ollama, a local AI runtime running on your own Mac. All AI processing happens on your device. No messages, prompts, or responses are sent to any external server.

The only network request Lokei makes is to your local Ollama server at localhost:11434 — which is your own computer, not the internet.

Local Storage

Lokei stores the following data locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults:

  • Your chat history
  • Your selected AI model preference
  • Your selected preset mode
  • App settings such as server URL and history limit

This data never leaves your device. You can clear your chat history at any time using the trash icon inside Lokei.

Third Party Services

Lokei does not integrate with any third party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. There are no SDKs, trackers, or embedded services of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Lokei does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is nothing to protect.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Since we collect no data, any future changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.
