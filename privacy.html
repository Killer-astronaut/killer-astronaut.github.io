PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated April 2026

Zero data collected by Lokei. Plain privacy, no account required.

The short version: Lokei collects absolutely nothing. With Apple Intelligence or local Ollama, your conversations and files stay on your Mac. If you choose to configure a remote provider, the messages you send to that provider leave your Mac and are governed by that provider's policy. Lokei still does not collect them.

1. What We Collect

Nothing. Lokei does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data on Lokei servers. Period.

Specifically, Lokei does not collect:

  • Your conversations or messages
  • Files or documents you share with Lokei
  • Your name, email, or any account information
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Crash reports sent to us
  • Location data
  • Any other personal information

2. How Lokei Works

Lokei is a Mac AI assistant with private local-first backends. On supported Macs, Apple Intelligence can process requests on device. With Ollama, Lokei talks to your local Ollama server running on your own machine, typically at localhost:11434.

You can also add OpenAI-compatible or other remote providers yourself. When you choose a remote provider, prompts and attachments sent to that provider leave your Mac and are governed by that provider's terms. Lokei does not operate a relay server and does not receive a copy.

3. Data Storage

Lokei stores your chat history and preferences locally on your Mac using macOS standard storage (UserDefaults and local files). This data:

  • Stays on your Mac by default
  • Is only accessible by you
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Can sync through your Apple iCloud account only if you enable iCloud sync

4. Third Party Services

Lokei does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. There are no SDKs, trackers, or beacons embedded in the app.

Lokei can use Apple Intelligence on supported Macs, local Ollama models, or providers you configure yourself. Apple, Ollama, and any remote provider you add have their own terms and privacy policies.

5. App Store Distribution

Lokei is being prepared for the Apple Mac App Store. Once listed there, Apple will handle payment processing and purchase records. Lokei will not receive your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs Apple's handling of purchase data.

6. Children's Privacy

Lokei does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to protect.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given that our policy is "we collect nothing," we don't anticipate meaningful changes.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? We're happy to answer them — though the answer to most data collection questions will be "we don't collect that."

Questions about privacy? Reach out anytime.
