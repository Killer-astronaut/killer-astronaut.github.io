What it does

A heartbeat away.

Everything in the well-loved apps people swear by — distilled, refined, and built native for Apple Watch with the Lokei privacy posture.

One-tap heart

Touch the complication on your watch face and your person feels a soft haptic on their wrist. No menus, no typing, no app launches.

Every complication style

Modular, Infograph, Corner, Circular, X-Large. Ships with all WidgetKit families so you can place Love Birds on any watch face.

Custom haptic patterns

Compose a signature buzz only the two of you know — three quick taps, a long pulse, a heartbeat rhythm. Inspired by Bond Touch.

Live pulse share

Press and hold to send your real heart rate as a haptic rhythm on their wrist. Watch-to-watch, never stored, never uploaded.

Heart vocabulary

Hearts, hugs, kisses, coffee breaks, goodnight, thinking of you, on my way home — 16 thoughtful "vibes" plus your own SF Symbols.

Streaks & memories

Days-in-a-row counter, a private timeline of every tap, and an "on this day a year ago" surface — borrowed from the best mood-tracking apps.

End-to-end encrypted

Routed through Apple's CloudKit private database with encrypted shared zones. Lokei never sees your hearts. There is no Lokei server.

StandBy & Lock Screen

Tap from your bedside StandBy view, the Lock Screen, or the Action Button. Live Activity shows incoming hearts on the Dynamic Island.

iMessage extension

Send the same animated hearts inside Messages so the love still works even when their watch is charging.

Family mode

Pair with multiple people — partner, kids, parents, best friend. Each one gets their own color, sound, and haptic signature.

Quiet hours & Focus

Honors Sleep, Work, Do Not Disturb, and custom Focus filters automatically. Set your own quiet hours per recipient.

Voice pings

Hold the Action Button to record a 5-second voice burst. Auto-deletes after they listen. Mic stays off until you press.

Important dates

Anniversaries, birthdays, the date you met. A subtle ring on the complication when the day arrives, with a private message ready to send.

Long-distance map (opt-in)

One private dot per person on a tiny world map. Coarse "home zone" only — never live tracking, never a route. Off by default.

Shared lists & photos

A little pinboard for "places to go," "movies to watch," and a rotating photo widget pulled from a shared iCloud album you both choose.

Apple Intelligence nudges

Optional gentle reminders on supported devices — "you haven't tapped in 3 days, want to say hi?" — generated on-device, never sent anywhere.

Accessible by default

Full VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion, color-blind palette, and an option to convert hearts to descriptive haptics only.

Apple-Native Stack

Built right on top of the platform.

No third-party SDKs, no analytics libraries, no JavaScript runtime in your pocket. Every brick is from Apple.

UI
SwiftUI on watchOS One codebase across watchOS, iOS, iPadOS, visionOS. ClockKit complications for legacy faces, WidgetKit timelines for modern ones.
Sync
CloudKit private & shared End-to-end encrypted shared CKDatabase zones between paired devices. Apple holds the keys path; Lokei never touches your messages.
Touch
Core Haptics Custom AHAP patterns let you sculpt the exact rhythm of a touch. Falls back gracefully on older Apple Watches.
Live
ActivityKit & App Intents Dynamic Island, Lock Screen Live Activities, Siri Shortcuts, and Action Button targets — all standard system surfaces.
Health
HealthKit (opt-in) Heart-rate pulse share reads a single live BPM during the press and forgets it. Nothing is written, stored, or correlated.
AI
Apple Intelligence (when available) On-device nudges and suggested vibes use Foundation Models locally. No prompts ever leave the device.
