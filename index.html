  let cleaned = string
    .lowercased()
    .filter { $0.isLetter }
  return cleaned == String(cleaned.reversed())

Why Lokei

Everything you need.

Built for focus, speed, and local-first privacy. Lokei works quietly in the background so you can stay in flow.

Private By Design

Use Apple Intelligence on supported Macs or local Ollama models for private, on-device work. Lokei collects no conversations, files, analytics, or account data.

Always In Your Menu Bar

Get answers in seconds without breaking your workflow. Lokei lives in your menu bar, ready the instant you need it — no app switching, no loading screens.

Streaming Responses

Read answers as they're generated, not after a long wait. Responses stream word by word from the backend you choose.

Syntax Highlighting

Copy-paste working code straight from the chat. Code renders with full syntax highlighting and one-click copy — Swift, Python, JavaScript and more.

Specialist Modes

Get better results for any task with purpose-built AI modes. Switch between Code Helper, Debug Mode, Writing Assistant, Brainstorm — or create your own.

Backend Choice

Choose Apple Intelligence, local Ollama models, or an OpenAI-compatible provider you configure. The AI & Models settings make the active backend clear.

Model Library

Find the right Ollama model for your Mac in seconds. Browse, compare, and download models right inside Lokei — no Terminal needed. Every model is rated for your RAM.

Project Context

Stop repeating yourself every conversation. Tell Lokei about your project once and it remembers — every response is already tailored to your work.

Chat History

Pick up right where you left off. Every conversation is saved automatically with smart titles — browse, resume, or export anytime.

Expand to Full Window

Need more room? Pop Lokei into a full resizable window with a sidebar for deep work. Collapse back to the menu bar when you're done.

Setup

Up and running in minutes.

1

Choose your backend

On supported Macs, start with Apple Intelligence for private on-device responses. Prefer open-source local models? Choose Ollama instead.

2

Install Lokei

Lokei is being prepared for the Mac App Store. Once installed, onboarding guides you through Apple Intelligence or Ollama setup based on what your Mac supports.

3

Pick a model if needed

Apple Intelligence needs no model download inside Lokei. For Ollama, open Settings → AI & Models to browse and download local models rated for your Mac's RAM.

4

Start asking

Click the Lokei leaf in your menu bar and start chatting. Local and on-device backends keep your work on your Mac; remote providers are only used if you configure one.
