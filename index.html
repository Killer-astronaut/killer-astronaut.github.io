The vision

Not just answers. The right kind of help.

Most AI waits for the perfect prompt. Lokei is being built around your working position: when you're stuck, it gets direct. When you're confused, it slows down. When you're building, it gives steps. When you're researching, it helps narrow the decision. When the work needs deeper reasoning, Advisor Mode can step in.

Predictive text guesses your words. Lokei is being built to predict what kind of help you need.

Advisor Mode · in development

A second brain, only when you need one.

Some questions deserve a second brain. Advisor Mode lets Lokei use a stronger model only when needed — for deeper reasoning, confidence checks, complex coding decisions, or high-stakes tradeoffs. Use a fast model for everyday work. Escalate to a stronger model when the moment calls for it.

Setup

Up and running in minutes.

1

Choose your backend

On supported Macs, start with Apple Intelligence for private on-device responses. Prefer open-source local models? Choose Ollama instead.

2

Install Lokei

Download Lokei from the Mac App Store. Onboarding guides you through Apple Intelligence or Ollama setup based on what your Mac supports.

3

Pick a model if needed

Apple Intelligence needs no model download inside Lokei. For Ollama, open Settings → AI & Models to browse and download local models rated for your Mac's RAM.

4

Start asking

Click the Lokei leaf in your menu bar and start chatting. Local and on-device backends keep your work on your Mac; remote providers are only used if you configure one.
