Borrowed Time — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Borrowed Time does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

What the app does on the network

The app requests public, anonymous data from the U.S. Treasury's FiscalData API (api.fiscaldata.treasury.gov) to display the national debt. These requests contain no personal information or identifiers. No other network requests are made by the app.

What stays on your device

App preferences (such as a count of tips given, used only to show a thank-you note) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage and never leave it.

In-app purchases

Optional tips are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Borrowed Time never sees your payment information. Tips unlock nothing and are never required.

Photographs

The monthly archive photographs are United States public-domain images from the National Archives, the Library of Congress, NASA, and the National Park Service, bundled with the app and credited in-app.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
